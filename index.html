Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets for an event? +

Booking tickets is easy! Simply browse our events page, click on the event you're interested in, then click "Book Now". Fill out the booking form with your details and select the number of tickets you need. You'll receive a confirmation email once your booking is complete.

Can I cancel or refund my ticket? +

Yes, you can cancel your tickets up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the event may be subject to a processing fee. To cancel, please contact our support team with your booking reference number.

How will I receive my tickets? +

After completing your booking, you'll receive an email confirmation with your digital tickets attached as a PDF. You can either print your tickets or show them on your mobile device at the event entrance.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and mobile money services (M-Pesa, Airtel Money). All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

What if an event is cancelled? +

In the rare event of cancellation, you will receive a full refund automatically within 5-7 business days. We'll also notify you immediately via email and SMS if you've provided your phone number.

Can I transfer my tickets to someone else? +

Yes, tickets can be transferred to another person up to 24 hours before the event. Please contact our support team with the new attendee's details, and we'll update the booking accordingly.
